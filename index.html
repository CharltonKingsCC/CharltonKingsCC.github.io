About CKCC

Charlton Kings Cricket Club, on the outskirts of Cheltenham, is a friendly community cricket club which has been designated a focus club by the ECB to be a hub of the community network for cricket development, particularly for the benefit of children and young people. We have achieved Clubmark Accreditation by demonstrating that best practices are in place to develop and deliver high quality community cricket in a safe, effective and child-friendly way.

Charlton Kings senior section runs 4 men’s teams on a Saturday, 3 Midweek League teams and a Women’s team. In 2015 our 1st XII were Gloucestershire Men’s County Trophy Winners and our Women's 1st XII were runners-up in the Cotswold District Cricket Association's Women's League.

The club has a thriving youth section with boys' teams at U9, U11 (GYL County Champions 2015), U13 (GYL County Champions 2015), U15 (GYL County runners-up 2015), U17 (GYL Champions 2015) and U19, and girls' teams at U11 and U13. The club takes great pride in developing cricketers through its youth programme into the senior sides.

Further information about the club including fixtures can be found on our play-cricket page: charltonkings.play-cricket.com
